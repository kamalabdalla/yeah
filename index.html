Top Destinations

Mnemba

The Island of Zanzibar is considered the best scuba diving and snorkeling location in the East Africa with Mnemba Island (1 hour boat ride away) providing the best spot. Many of the sites surrounding the Island are colorful and thriving, making for fun snorkeling at any time of year, Expect to see lion fish, moray eels, stingrays and turtles swimming around in the shallows as well as humpback dolphin while are sailing to Island. You will be provided with masks and fins but you are able to Use our own equipment. You will stay out for 5-6 hours Starting at shun, After snorkeling at Mnemba we provided a barbecue at a nearby beach before we head back home under the power of the dhow sail (winds permitting)

Jambiani

Kuza Cave in Jambian , East Coast Zanzibar offers a unique experience for adventurous nature-lovers, snorkellers, divers and people interested in Swahili Culture. the cave is a 50 metre round sink hole in the middle od a small bridge and walk down a few stairs to enter an underground oasis. thecircular shape of the cave is a result of over 250,000 years of rainwater eroding the limestone, A large natural rock sculpture in the shape of an elephant is used by locals as alter to make offering to the spirits who live in the cave and request their support.

Michanvi

The Rock Restaurant, In michanvi pingwe on Zanzibar Island, is an extraordinary Restaurant, located in a unique position on a rock in the middle of the Indian Ocean. the contrast of the unspoiled natural setting, down played decor and design vs the international standard food preparation and presentation is as novel and rewardig as its location. serving up to 14 tables. all facing a wonderfull terrace on the Ocean. the Restaurant is connected to the mainland by boat, within high tide, and reachable by foot within low tide. It is also easy reachable by sea if coming by boat from an excursion. Perfect for romantic dinner for two or for a memorable evening with friend or family, it is open for lunch and dinner and is available to be reversed for small groups and ceremonies. The Rock kitchen offers a superlative fresh seafood menu, including vegetarian plates and a kids menu the service is informal but accurant and elegant.

Nungwi Village tour

Nungwi is a village at the northern end of the Tanzanian island of Unguja, familiarly called Zanzibar. It's known for the wide, palm-lined Nungwi Beach and a nearby boat-building yard for the production of traditional dhows. The Mnarani Marine Turtle Conservation Pond shelters green and hawksbill sea turtles in a tidal lagoon. Off the coast, coral reefs teeming with colorful fish include Hunga and Leven Bank
